# Code of Conduct

StealthHumanizer follows the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/) Code of Conduct (v2.1).

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project and community harassment-free for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Provide and accept constructive feedback.
- Prioritize community health and long-term maintainability.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation.
- Personal attacks, trolling, or insulting comments.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Any conduct that is inappropriate in a professional open-source setting.

## Scope

This policy applies in all community spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and project communications.

## Reporting and Enforcement

Report incidents to **maintainers via GitHub private security advisory or direct maintainer contact**. Maintainers will review reports promptly, maintain confidentiality where possible, and determine fair corrective action.
